THE MIRACLE CHURCH MINISTRIES INTERNATIONAL


     The Miracle Church Ministries International was established in the year 1979 in the town city of Ulhasnagar (Maharashtra) under the fearful & leading voice of Holy Spirit. late.REV.A.D.SAMUEL was the founder of the Miracle Church Ministries. The church was named as MIRACLE by seeing the continuous miracles of God in his life and people.
     Later on after the death of Rev. A.D. Samuel in the year 2007, His elder son REV.D.S.TITUS is called by a clear call of holy spirit  for fulfilling the vison towards the church   
  The Miracle Church is still here to SERVING THE WILL OF GOD! (Glorifying JESUS CHRIST)

 THE LIFE OF REV.A.D. SAMUEL
       (The founder of MCMI)

 
REV.A.D. SAMUEL
(1954-2007)

       Rev. A.D. Samuel the great man of God born on 18th April 1954 in Thiruvalla  of Kerala State. From childhood, He was called for the ministry of God in schools and college he used to preach the word of God. He started church in Othera and also in cochin (Kerala) in 1978.
       From 1980 he started serving in the State of Maharashtra for 28 years. Also he visited the Gulf Countries in 1994 for sharing the word of God
